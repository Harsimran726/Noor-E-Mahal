Inspired by the grandeur of Punjabi royalty, Noor E Mahal stands as a testament to architectural excellence. Its domes, arches, and intricate jali work echo the splendour of an era defined by princely courts and royal celebrations.

Where Every Moment is Royal

From the moment you step through the grand entrance, you are enveloped in an atmosphere of regal sophistication. Crystal chandeliers illuminate marble halls, while hand-crafted details reflect centuries of artisan tradition.

Whether grand ceremonies in the main hall or intimate gatherings in the royal gardens, every space within Noor E Mahal has been designed to create unforgettable memories.

A Legacy Continuing

With a dedicated team of event planners, master chefs, and hospitality professionals, Noor E Mahal ensures that your celebration is nothing short of legendary.

Our commitment to excellence has made us Punjab's most sought-after destination for weddings, receptions, and grand celebrations of all kinds.

A Palace Built for Love

Every brick, every dome, and every garden path at Noor E Mahal was crafted with one purpose — to create the most magical setting for the most important day of your life.
